About Me

Welcome to my first webpage! I am currently in the UCLA Web Development Coding Bootcamp and am so excited to be on this journey. It has been a wirlwind of a first two weeks, and they were right.... Already I look back at this first webpage and realized how far I've come, and just how much I learned.

This website was a struggle to start but an ease and joy to finish. Through my experience I have learned how much I love the process of coding, and that most solutions are, in hindsight, simple "fixes":

  • Do I have a closing /div > ?
  • Is there an extra comma somewhere?
  • Are the proper style.css and logic.js scripts linked?
  • Do I have a "." for classes and "#" fir Ids on my CSS sheet?
    • ...And so many more!

But this week I learned about Bootstrap! Bootstrap is an incredible tool, however, I love that I learned about the fundamentals first without knowing about Bootstrap; without having struggled through this first webpage, I would not only not appreciate the power of Bootstrap, but I wouldn't understand how a page works together (!) or any of the more advanced concepts we're starting to learn about on as deep a level.
